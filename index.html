Why was Pinecone created?

In short Pinecone was created as a free for personal use responsive website theme.

Who Created Pinecone?

A Multimedia Designer named Anthony Aguayo got bored one day while at home. He wanted to create a theme that would be simple, responsive, and easy to edit for people.
